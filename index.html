ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Ivan Kent P. Parajes

DIGITAL ARTIST / ANIMATION STUDENT

A digital artist and graphic artist based in Goa, Camarines Sur. I'm also an animation student at Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology, where I have the opportunity to develop my skills and learn various animation techniques and software. I am constantly honing my craft through commissions and personal projects. My ultimate goal is to create my own animated film and series, where I can share my stories and explore diverse ethnic cultures.

Through animation, I want to showcase different backgrounds and perspectives, fostering inclusivity and opening doors for underrepresented voices. Join me on my artistic journey as I continue to develop my skills, learn new animation techniques, and create captivating storytelling experiences. Together, let's challenge norms and contribute to a more inclusive entertainment industry. Thank you for your support, and I invite you to explore my portfolio and be part of this exciting creative adventure.
